our story, His story

"We love because He first loved us." (1 John 4:19).

We first met in 2013, when we were going to university in Cambridge. It was an exciting time of learning and exploration for us, and we became fast friends. Through Christian fellowship, we experienced God's boundless love for us, and began to understand what it means to love unconditionally. What an amazing time of growth!

But it wasn't until 2015 that we got together. Then, Yi Le ambitiously declared that we were on a 3-year plan to be wed...

Evidently, we've taken a little longer than that. Through our journey together, we've discovered that two very different people can have very different ideas. Sometimes it's hard enough agreeing on what to do for the day - let alone what to do with a lifetime together! At our lowest point, we even wondered if our relationship could survive the trials of life.

Thankfully, when that happened, we could look back on why we embarked on this adventure together. God's love was what brought us together, and it is God's love that will take us through, till death we do part. We draw encouragement from the thought that "unless God builds the house, the builders build in vain" (Psalm 127:1).

We invite you to join us as we take our vows - and to remind us that love and forgiveness aren't always easy, but they're worth it.
