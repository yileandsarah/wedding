our story, His story

Opposites attract, but "love covers over a multitude of sins" (1 Peter 4:8). We first met in 2013, when we were going to university in Cambridge. It was an exciting time of learning and exploration for us, and we became fast friends.

But it wasn't till 2015 that we got together. Then, Yi Le declared that we were on a 3-year plan to be wed... we've taken a little longer than that!

Along the way, we discovered that marriage is hard work. Sometimes it's hard enough to decide what to do with our time together - let alone what to do with a lifetime together.

At our lowest point, we even wondered if our relationship could survive the trials of life. But that was when we were taught our most important lesson so far: "unless God builds the house, the builders build in vain" (Psalm 127:1).

We invite you to join us as we take our vows - and to remind us that love and forgiveness aren't always easy, but they're worth it.
